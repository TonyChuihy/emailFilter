Dear Students,

Are you passionate about marketing, social media, and storytelling?
Ready to showcase your talents and turn your creative ideas into real-world impact?

The Undergraduate Recruitment and Admissions Office (URAO) is excited to invite you to join the HKUST Marketer Talent Program — a fantastic opportunity to be part of a dynamic team of marketing and digital content creators on campus. Whether you are a video editor, copywriting enthusiast, or social media strategist, this is your perfect chance to gain hands-on experience, enhance your skills, and expand your professional network.

Role/ Responsibilities:

  • Showcase HKUST’s innovative programs and vibrant university life through marketing campaigns both on-campus and online.
  • Create captive content like reels, shorts, student stories, and interactive activities to connect with our target audience (high school students and their parents)
  • Design eye-catching visuals and edit videos for social media posts on Instagram, Facebook, Xiaohongshu, WeChat, etc.
  • Craft compelling copy for marketing materials.
  • Support the on-site execution of marketing events and outreach initiatives.

Perks:

  • Professional Development: Acquire hands-on marketing experience and sharpen your editing skills.
  • Exclusive Insights: Get behind-the-scenes knowledge of higher education marketing strategies.
  • Career Boost: Obtain personalized recommendation letters and certificates for your career planning.
  • Earn While You Learn: Get paid for creating compelling content, designing visuals, editing work, and supporting our dynamic events.

 

What to expect:

  • Regular team gathering for sharing marketing trends and idea brainstorming.
  • Guidance on essential marketing skills and strategies by our experienced marketing team.
  • Real campaign ownership – your content may reach thousands of prospective students!

 

Requirements:

  • Open to all HKUST undergraduates from any major (marketing or business majors preferred)
  • Demonstrates creativity, self-motivation, proactiveness, and a willingness to learn
  • Proficiency in Canva and mobile/video editing apps (e.g., CapCut, iMovie); experience with Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator/Premiere Pro is a strong advantage.
  • Bilingual (English & Chinese) copywriting skills are highly valued.
  • Experience in managing social media accounts is a plus

Application Method:

To apply for the program, please complete the following steps by the deadline of 23:59, Nov 30, 2025 (Sunday):

  1. Fill out the online application form with your personal details.
  2. Submit your portfolio showcasing your marketing, design, and editing skills as well as your passion. This portfolio could include blog posts, videos, drawings, visual designs, and links to your social media accounts.

Selected applicants may be invited to a casual chat to further discuss their interest and eligibility for the program. The result will be released by early December.

 

Don't miss out on this chance if you are passionate about marketing and eager to expand your skills and knowledge. Apply now and delve deeper into the marketing field!
