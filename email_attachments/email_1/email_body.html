SSCI International UG Exchange Program (Fall 2026) is now open for applications.

 

About the Program

To know more about the Program, please visit our webpage Outgoing International Exchange Program. You may also get some tips on making your exchange choices wisely and know more about the application details from the PPT slides of our info session.

 

Application Method

Complete ALL the following parts by 23:59 HKT, 30 Nov 2025 (Sun):

  1. Apply via My Study Abroad System at https://mystudyabroad.hkust.edu.hk/
  2. Update ‘My Profile’ at mySCI at https://sdb.science.ust.hk/mySCI (Guideline)

 

Incomplete or late application will not be considered.


Special Notes

  • The School targets to announce the application results by late January/early February. Email notification will be sent to applicants when results are released.
  • For BIBU / BTGBM / DSCT / EVMT/ IIM / MAEC / RMBI students, please be reminded of the course restrictions set by the Science exchange partners (if any). Please read carefully the Special Notes to your major under ‘Learning Content’ in the My Study Abroad System before applying.
